# Product contract

## The job

Turn structured facts about a scripted feature or series into the smallest useful deck plan for the next real Film and TV decision.

## Inputs

Quick mode uses the project form, the deck’s immediate job, the first recipient, how it travels, and what already exists. Expert mode may add stage, request status, authorship, attachments, evidence, rights, access, finance, production, and delivery conditions.

## Outputs

- prerequisites that come before deck work;
- an ordered set of page jobs with plain reasons;
- items to reuse, gather, build, park, or verify;
- assumptions made by the quick route;
- a horizontal 16:9 plan preview.

## Boundaries

Deck Bones does not write the deck, validate creative quality, invent evidence, promise access, clear rights, calculate finance, or replace current written requirements and qualified professional advice. The quick route may use explicitly labeled conservative assumptions; expert mode exists to replace those assumptions with choices.

## Privacy

Selections and results remain in the browser. The production tool has no account, database, analytics, upload, or runtime AI call.
